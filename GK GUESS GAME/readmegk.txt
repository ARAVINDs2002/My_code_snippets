# General Knowledge (GK) Quiz

The General Knowledge (GK) Quiz is a simple C program that allows users to answer multiple-choice questions related to various topics. The program presents questions and evaluates the user's answers to provide a score.

## Introduction

The GK Quiz program is designed to test your knowledge on a variety of topics. It presents a series of multiple-choice questions and evaluates your answers. The program provides immediate feedback on whether your answer is correct or incorrect and keeps track of your score.
